DONATIONS

  • Who is going to find the cure for cancer
  • Who is going to stop the bombings and bring sense to this world?
  • Who is going create the social space for all us regardless of race, colour or religion?

Well, as you ponder the answers, you must be thinking of what kind of a person that will be. That will be a person or people ( children, youth or adults) with the potential to see things in 100 years to come , in 100 different ways and do have the courage to dare it come through.

Such are the people we are dealing with and we have to ensure that they are supported and made to realize their potentials.

Our efforts and desire to make this work, is hampered by poverty in Africa. We cannot allow poverty to kill the potential of a person who can make this world better. We must therefore pull in resources together to make this noble objective become a reality.

As an affiliate of the World Talent Federation we have the huge task of making this happen in Africa and with all the poverty! We have to support them uniquely and differently, using resources and experts . This requires funds and we may not be able to have enough to support all genres of talent.

Every year, we have several efforts for poor people that include FREE Talent Scouting, Talent Identifications and Screenings, Talent Development Programmes and Talent Based Learning. It further includes providing scholarships to this needy group to complete their learning at university levels.

We further have to help create FREE Gift and Talent Clinics, with donated equipment, and support experts in these clinics. We do this for the noble reason that the next gifted person, who may change this world, may just be from the slums!

Finally, when giving to an effective charity, like ours, the size of your donation directly correlates with the number of people you are able to help. But you don’t have to be a millionaire to make a significant difference; even small donations have the potential to drastically improve an individual’s quality of life.

If you or your organization wishes to support our efforts please send to us an email: donate@giftedafrica.org
