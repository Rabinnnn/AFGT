TALENT SCHOLARSHIPS

Despite the volumes of talent that we witness in Africa, most of it gets washed down the drain for lack of financial support

The African Federation for Gifted and Talented will avail, in September of any year, such funding to deserving applicants.

In order to be considered, you will need to apply with testimonials that indicate:

  • 1. Evidence (s) of your unique Talent
  • 2. Why you need the funding
  • 3. Efforts you have made on your own before seeking funding from us

Notes: Please note that we fund unique talent and NOT the usual academic scholarships for the sake of completing academics
